# Prezi structure map — The Grand Compromise

How to assemble the zoom structure in Prezi after importing the slides.
Time estimate: ~15 minutes of arranging + topic styling.

## Recommended Prezi template family
Pick any template with a **left-to-right journey/timeline overview** (Prezi calls these
"journey" layouts). The overview canvas should read as a path — that is our timeline.

## Topic structure

```
OVERVIEW (the canvas — a left-to-right journey)
│
├── [no topic — standalone intro slide] 1. Kickoff
│
├── TOPIC A: "The Compromises"            ← gold/ochre topic color
│   ├── 2. Why are you sitting like this?
│   ├── 3. Compromise I — The Lecture
│   ├── 4. Compromise II — Batch Processing
│   ├── 5. Compromise III — The Exam Machine
│   └── 6. And it worked.                 ← make this the LAST subtopic, then zoom
│                                            back to overview after it (Prezi does
│                                            this automatically when a topic ends —
│                                            this is our "first full zoom-out")
│
├── TOPIC B: "The Measurement & the Break"  ← place visually ABOVE the path if the
│   ├── 7. The 2 Sigma Problem               template allows free placement
│   ├── 8. What just became cheap
│   ├── 9. What breaks — precisely
│   └── 10. It's not autocomplete anymore
│
├── TOPIC C: "The Reveal"                  ← dark topic cover if possible
│   ├── 11. THE REVEAL (terminal)           (you switch to the real terminal here)
│   └── 12. The agent couldn't grade itself
│
├── TOPIC D: "The Undrawn Future"          ← blue topic color, place at far right
│   ├── 13. What's scarce now
│   ├── 14. Sketches, not solutions
│   └── 15. Open questions                  ← 4 question sub-bullets; in Prezi make
│                                             each question a separate subtopic
│                                             scattered around the topic cover
│
└── [standalone closing slide] 16. Closing line
    Then press the overview button — leave the FULL CANVAS visible for discussion.
```

## Critical staging notes (carry over from the design)
- Slide 1 (Kickoff): you leave Prezi, start the agent in a terminal, come back.
  Practice the app switch (Cmd+Tab) — Prezi Present keeps your position.
- After slide 6, Prezi's automatic zoom-back-to-overview IS the dramatic beat —
  pause there, let the audience see the whole journey.
- Slide 11: switch to the real terminal for the live reveal, then return.
- After the closing slide, hit the overview/home button and leave it on screen
  during the discussion so the audience can re-read the open questions.

## What Prezi import will NOT carry over
- Speaker notes from the .pptx may not import — copy them per-slide into Prezi's
  presenter notes (they are all in the .pptx notes panes and in prezi-ai-brief.md).
- The fonts (Fraunces / IBM Plex Mono) — pick Prezi's closest serif + monospace.
- The reveal/verify slides' terminal styling — Prezi has code-block-ish text frames;
  approximate or screenshot the styled step from index.html and paste as image.
